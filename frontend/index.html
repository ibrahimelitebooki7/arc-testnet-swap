How to Swap on Xroga

1

Install a Web3 wallet like MetaMask or Rabby.

2

Click “Connect Wallet” and switch to Arc Testnet (automatically added).

3

If you have no testnet tokens, click the “Faucet” button to get free USDC and EURC.

4

Enter the amount, choose tokens (USDC ↔ EURC), and click “Swap tokens”.

5

Confirm the transaction in your wallet – the swap will complete in seconds.
